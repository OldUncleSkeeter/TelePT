About TheraLeap

Theraleap is a platform for remote Physical Therapy. Patients recovering from injury or operation of the arms and hands will now have the ablity to perform their routine's in the comfort of their home.

Programmed in Python, using Leap motion.

Delevoped by: Karla Montejo, Andres Rodriguez, Drew Collins, and Christopher Montejo
